We introduce Foveation-based Explanations (FovEx), a novel human-inspired
visual explainability (XAI) method for Deep Neural Networks. Our method
achieves state-of-the-art performance on both transformer (on 4 out of 5
metrics) and convolutional models (on 3 out of 5 metrics), demonstrating its
versatility. Furthermore, we show the alignment between the explanation map
produced by FovEx and human gaze patterns (+14% in NSS compared to RISE,
+203% in NSS compared to gradCAM), enhancing our confidence in FovEx's ability
to close the interpretation gap between humans and machines.
