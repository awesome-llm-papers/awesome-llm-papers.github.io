Cartoon videos have proven to be effective in learning vocabulary to
preschool children.However, we have little knowledge about integrating AI into
cartoon videos to provide systematic, multimodal vocabulary learning support.
This late-breaking work present \name\{\}, an AI-powered cartoon video system
that enables real-time Q\&A, vocabulary review, and contextual learning.
Preliminary findings contextualized how families interact with \name\{\} to
support vocabulary learning. Parents appreciated the system for its
personalized, engaging experiences, fostering collaboration, and encouraging
self-reflection on parenting. This study offers valuable design implications
for informing future video systems to support vocabulary learning.
