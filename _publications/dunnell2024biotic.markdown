This paper presents "Biotic Browser," an innovative AI assistant leveraging
StreamingLLM to transform web navigation and task execution. Characterized by
its ability to simulate the experience of a passenger in an autonomous vehicle,
the Biotic Browser excels in managing extended interactions and complex,
multi-step web-based tasks. It marks a significant advancement in AI
technology, particularly in the realm of long-term context management, and
offers promising applications for enhancing productivity and efficiency in both
personal and professional settings.
